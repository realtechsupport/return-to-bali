The bali-26 dataset contains images of 26 categories of ethnobotanically significant trees, plants, fruits and seeds. Each category is represented by between 1000 and 2500 images. All images in this collection were collected on the Island of Bali in the vicinity of Ubud, specifically in the villages of Penglipuran, Kerta, Jatiluwih, Buahan, Sekaan and Bayung Gede between the months of late February and May 2020, with some additions during the spring of 2021.

Download the image dataset (4GB): bali-26 collection, release 1.0 (4GB)

Special thanks to Gusti Sutarjana, his family, and Made Darmaja for sharing their knowledge.
